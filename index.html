Meet The Fighter

Giovanni Vance Errisson-Johnson

Mofire the Super Namekian

  • Born: November 2, 1986
  • Gym: Empire Mixed Martial Arts
  • Height: 6’1’’
  • Weight Class: 135/145 lbs
  • Disciplines: Sanda (Chinese kickboxing), Muay Thai, Dutch kickboxing, Boxing, BJJ, Judo
  • Sanda record: 7-0
  • MMA record: 2-3
  • Muay Thai record: 2-1
  • K1 rules: 1-0
With a budding interest in Mixed Martial Arts that began during his childhood, Bahamian-born Giovanni jumped at the opportunity to train in taekwondo in 2009. Shortly thereafter, he began expanding his skill set, adding Judo, Jui Jitsu, Boxing and Sanda, the base of his fighting style.
Currently a 4x International Chinese Martial Arts Championship (ICMAC) World Sanda League champion, Giovanni first competed in 2010, participating in the Bahamas Open Martial Arts Championship (BOMAC) earning a gold medal in Sanda.
Ready to take on the challenge of MMA fighting, Giovanni started training with The Junkyard Dogs in Arlington, TX where he began incorporating wrestling into his fighting style. He made his MMA debut in 2012 at Renaissance MMA, a popular MMA promotion in New Orleans.
Although he would lose that fight, Giovanni continued with his training, which paid off in a win by unanimous decision at his second MMA fight at Real Cage Fights, in Melbourne, FL.Giovanni also trained at Sityodtong Muay Thai Academy in Somerville, MA for six months, adding Muay Thai to his fighting style.
He has since competed twice in Combat Night in Fort Lauderdale, FL, both losses. Known for his patience and accuracy, Giovanni uses his fast hands and quick footwork to show off his range of fighting styles.
