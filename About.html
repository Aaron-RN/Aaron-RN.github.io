Who I Am

I am a graphic designer based in the Bahamas with a love of graffiti and Japanese styled art.


My works often include straight lines, bright colors, and ‘clean’ (symmetrical) designs.


I've studied Fine Arts and Graphic Design in Boston at the School of the Museum of Fine Arts (SMFA) and the New England Institute of Art.


Some of my works include designs created for Compass Point Beach Resort, the Beast Mode fight team and many other local and international companies.

What I Do

  • Logo Design
  • Photo Editing
  • Illustrations
  • Promotional Designs
